Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a baked Italian dish consisting of wide strips of pasta cooked and layered with meat or vegetables, cheese, and tomato sauce

Ingredients

  • Sweet Italian sausage & lean ground beef
  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomoato products
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasonings
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheeses
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe insructions
  5. Cover the foil and bake
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving
